About Us

Where it all began

Founded on July 5, 2002, by automotive enthusiast Jacob P de Dios, our passion for Japanese Domestic Market (JDM) custom body parts and kits drives everything we do. With years of expertise and an unwavering commitment to quality, we have become a trusted source for automotive customization. Our mission is to provide fellow enthusiasts with access to the finest JDM-inspired aftermarket parts, enabling you to transform your vehicle into a unique masterpiece.
